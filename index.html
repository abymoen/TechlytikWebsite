Omicron - "An open solution, for an open world"

Introducing Omicron.
The Techlytik open source well monitoring system.

Designed for integrating into customers existing SCADA and plant monitoring systems or as a stand a lone unit. The choice is yours. .

Powerful simplicity

Easily connection to device over 3G-HSPA and 4G-LTE cellular networks, also included is Wi-Fi with mesh networking.

Versitile Performance

Able to connect to a variety of manufacturers 400 Mhz and 900 Mhz radio networks. Supported manufacturers include Schneider Electric, General Electric, Microhard, Freewave with more to come.

Accurate monotring

Monitor the various conditions of the pump jack including run state, belt slippage, gearbox vibration, strokes per minute, and weight imbalance.

Device Features

Integration

Integrate into any plant HMI or SCADA system or operate it as a independent device.

Mobile accessibility

Easy setup through our smart phone app

Simple Installation

Simply peel and press onto the weight arm of the veritcal arm of the pump jack

Battery backup

Two year battery life backup* (*pending customer use).

Self charging

On board kinetic energy generator. No need to replace batteries. Charge by the motion of the device.

LED state indication

Clear LED indication capable of flashing or pulsing various patterns and colors for local operating states.

Our partners

Flexible enough to connect to any system.

Mobile Application

Easily setup and monitor your devices with the Techlytik App.

The Techlytik app makes the simplest device setup possible. Register. Scan. Setup. Done.

The Omicron positive feedback LED’s indicate when a configuration update has been received. Monitor your sites with either the app or your operating systems. Or use both. The choice is yours. Configure your operator runs, set alarm limits, configure call out delays all at a touch.

Easily share your companies setup well monitor lists with others operators or technicians by adding them to your organizations user list that is storedin the cloud. Setup your device with cellular, radio serial or Ethernet networks, configure VPN’s, or setup with static IP’s on private networks. Visit the App store or Goggle Play to download the Techlytik app.

Available on Android and apple.
